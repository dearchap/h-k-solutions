## Solution

Let the terms of the arithmetic progression be $a, a+d, a+2d, ..., a+(n-1)d, .... , a+(2n-1)d, ...a+(3n-1)d$

Sum of the first $k$ terms $= a+(a+d)+(a+2d)+...+(a+(k-1)d) = ka + d(1+2+3+4+5...k-1) = ka+d(k-1)k/2$
